# Product

## Register

product

## Users
CEO of a hospital, healthcare directors, and administrative executives. They need a high-level, clear overview of hospital operations, patient metrics, and efficiency trends without being overwhelmed by technical clutter.

## Product Purpose
An operational intelligence dashboard that gives hospital executives immediate, clear insights into hospital performance, patient flow, resource utilization, and department health to make data-driven management decisions.

## Brand Personality
Clean, professional, minimal, and trustworthy. Inspired by Stripe and Notion. Focused on data precision, elegant layouts, and clear typographic hierarchy.

## Anti-references
- Overly flashy/distracting styles, heavy glassmorphism, or neon colors on dark backgrounds.
- Cluttered, dense tables with no visual hierarchy or spacing.
- AI or SaaS dashboard clichés (such as colored side-stripe borders, generic identical card grids, or useless illustrations).

## Design Principles
- **Immediate Executive Utility**: Highlight critical hospital health indicators (e.g., bed occupancy, ER wait times, staff levels) clearly at the top, ensuring they are readable in under 5 seconds.
- **Sleek Data Density**: Balance white space with clear information design so that data feels readable rather than sparse or overwhelming.
- **Trust via Restraint**: Use a clean, limited color palette where colors are only used to denote status (normal, warning, critical) or distinct categories.
- **Responsive Simplicity**: Maintain a grid system that adapts seamlessly across monitors and tablets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliant contrast ratios across light and dark modes.
- Clear, readable typography (sans-serif with appropriate line heights).
- Non-reliant on color alone (combining status colors with text labels or clear icons for status changes).
